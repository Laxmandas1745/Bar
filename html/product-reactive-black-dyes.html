Product Overview

Reactive Black dyes are widely used in the textile industry for dyeing cotton, viscose, linen, and other cellulosic fibers. These dyes form a strong covalent bond with the fiber, resulting in excellent wash fastness, deep shades, and superior color stability.

Our Reactive Black dyes are manufactured with strict quality control to ensure high color strength, excellent solubility, consistent shade reproduction, high fixation rate, and excellent fastness properties.

Customer Benefits

  • Deep Black Shades – Produces strong, rich black tones with excellent depth
  • Excellent Wash Fastness – Strong chemical bonding ensures color durability after multiple washes
  • High Fixation Rate – Maximizes dye utilization and reduces dye wastage
  • Consistent Shade Reproducibility – Ensures reliable color matching in production batches
  • Good Solubility – Allows easy preparation of dye solutions without filtration issues
  • Cost Efficient Processing – High fixation and reduced effluent load help lower processing costs

Application Methods

Exhaust Dyeing

Used for dyeing fabrics in dyeing machines where the dye solution gradually exhausts onto the fiber.

Cold Pad Batch (CPB)

A fabric padding method followed by batch fixation at room temperature, suitable for large-scale production.

Continuous Dyeing

Used in industrial processing where fabrics pass continuously through dye baths.

Textile Printing

Reactive dyes can also be used for textile printing to produce sharp and durable prints.
