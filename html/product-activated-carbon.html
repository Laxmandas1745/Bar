Product Overview

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of powdered, granulated, and impregnated Activated Carbon in India. Our company has established a strong presence across multiple application sectors of the activated carbon industry and continuously strives to deliver high-grade, reliable carbon solutions to global markets.

Activated carbon is produced from natural materials such as coconut shells, peat, hardwood, softwood, lignite coal, bituminous coal, olive pits, and other specialty materials. Also known as Activated Charcoal, it is widely recognized as a broad-spectrum adsorbent used in numerous industrial and environmental applications.

Due to its high porosity, extensive surface area, and strong surface reactivity, activated carbon is extremely effective in separation and purification processes, catalytic reactions, biomedical applications, environmental protection and pollution control.

Product Types

1. Coconut Shell Activated Carbon

Produced from coconut husk using a vapor activation process. Environmentally friendly, naturally sourced, and has a low carbon footprint. Its micro-pore molecular structure allows it to effectively absorb small contaminants, making it ideal for drinking water purification, air and gas purification, gold recovery, and industrial filtration processes.

2. Coal Activated Carbon

Manufactured from bituminous coal, anthracite, and lignite. Bituminous coal-based activated carbon is widely preferred due to its higher micro-porosity and stronger mechanical strength. Performs effectively in high-concentration applications and offers advantages such as lower cost and greater availability.

3. Water Washed Activated Carbon

Water washing removes excess ash content from granular activated carbon, improving its purity and performance. Available in various mesh sizes, customized according to customer requirements.

4. Acid Washed Activated Carbon

Acid washing removes mineral impurities and reduces ash content. Results in high-purity activated carbon with superior adsorption characteristics. Features extensive internal pore structure, neutral surface chemistry, maximum hardness and durability, and high activity with rapid pH stabilization.

5. Wood Activated Carbon

Produced from carefully selected wood sources through controlled carbonization and activation processes. Offers high purity and well-developed pore structure, making it particularly suitable for water filtration, food and beverage purification, deodorization, and pharmaceutical applications.

Properties

  • Extremely high porosity
  • Surface area ranging from 500 to 3000 m²/g
  • Highly developed internal pore structure
  • Diverse surface chemistry
  • Strong adsorption capability for pollutants
  • Effective removal of impurities from liquids and gases
