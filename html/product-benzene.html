Product Overview

Benzene (C₆H₆) is an aromatic hydrocarbon and one of the primary chemical building blocks for the petrochemical industry. It is widely used as a raw material for the production of numerous petrochemical intermediates and downstream products.

Benzene is typically obtained as a by-product from refinery processes, naphtha crackers, paraxylene production units, and steel manufacturing plants. It can also be produced through hydrodealkylation of toluene.

Our manufacturing facilities produce high-purity benzene suitable for petrochemical and industrial applications. Benzene is extensively used in the manufacture of key chemicals such as ethylbenzene, cumene, cyclohexane, and nitrobenzene.

Key Applications

Benzene is an important feedstock in the petrochemical industry and is used in the production of:

  • Ethylbenzene (styrene and polystyrene production)
  • Cumene (phenol and acetone production)
  • Cyclohexane (nylon production)
  • Nitrobenzene (aniline and dye manufacturing)
  • Maleic anhydride (polyester resins)
  • Alkylbenzene (detergents)
  • Chlorobenzenes
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Dyes and pigments
  • Pesticides and resins

Safety & Handling

Important Safety Information

Benzene is classified as a highly flammable liquid and hazardous substance. It is carcinogenic (Category 1A) and mutagenic (Category 1B) and must be handled with appropriate safety precautions.

Handling Precautions:
  • Store in cool, well-ventilated areas
  • Keep away from heat, sparks, and open flames
  • Use explosion-proof equipment
  • Prevent static discharge
  • Use appropriate protective equipment and ventilation systems
Required PPE:
  • Chemical safety goggles or face shield
  • Chemical-resistant gloves
  • Protective clothing
  • Respiratory protection where ventilation is insufficient
