# Reactive Black Dyes

## High Performance Reactive Black Dyes for Textile Applications

Reactive Black dyes are widely used in the textile industry for dyeing **cotton, viscose, linen, and other cellulosic fibers**. These dyes form a strong covalent bond with the fiber, resulting in **excellent wash fastness, deep shades, and superior color stability**.

Our Reactive Black dyes are manufactured with strict quality control to ensure:

- High color strength
- Excellent solubility
- Consistent shade reproduction
- High fixation rate
- Excellent fastness properties

These dyes are suitable for **exhaust dyeing, continuous dyeing, cold pad batch dyeing, and textile printing processes**.

# Application Methods

Reactive Black dyes can be applied using several textile dyeing techniques.

## Exhaust Dyeing
Used for dyeing fabrics in dyeing machines where the dye solution gradually exhausts onto the fiber.

## Cold Pad Batch (CPB)
A fabric padding method followed by batch fixation at room temperature, suitable for large-scale production.

## Continuous Dyeing
Used in industrial processing where fabrics pass continuously through dye baths.

## Textile Printing
Reactive dyes can also be used for textile printing to produce sharp and durable prints.
