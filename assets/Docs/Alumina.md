# Refractory Alumina Solutions

## Overview

High-performance alumina materials designed for refractory applications offer excellent heat resistance, chemical stability, and mechanical durability. These materials are widely used in furnaces, casting systems, and high-temperature industrial processes where reliability and thermal performance are critical.

Carefully controlled production parameters such as particle size distribution, purity, crystal structure, and surface characteristics ensure consistent quality across different grades of alumina used in refractory applications.

These materials support the production of high-performance refractory products including castables, ramming masses, and high-alumina cement systems.

---

## Key Benefits

- Superior high-temperature resistance and thermal stability  
- Consistent quality through controlled manufacturing processes  
- Tailored particle size distribution for specific refractory applications  
- Strong chemical stability in aggressive environments

# Flame Retardant Solutions

## Overview

Alumina Trihydrate (ATH) is widely used as a high-performance halogen-free flame-retardant additive across multiple industries. It helps enhance fire safety while maintaining material performance and processing efficiency.

ATH grades are engineered to meet stringent fire and smoke resistance standards according to globally accepted testing protocols such as UL94 V0, EN45545, IS 15061, UL 546, UL 723, and ASTM E84.

These materials are manufactured with controlled particle size distribution, surface properties, and flow characteristics to ensure reliable performance across demanding industrial applications.

---

## Applications

- HFFR cables
- Cable compounds
- Polymer composites
- Electrical and electronic components
- Paints and coatings
- Aluminium composite panels
- Batteries
- Adhesives

---

- Halogen-free formulation for safer applications
- Good thermal conductivity
- Improved application development capabilities
- Sustainable and environmentally responsible material solution

# Battery Solutions

## Overview

Advanced alumina materials play an important role in modern battery technologies, particularly in lithium-ion battery systems. These materials are used in battery separators, electrode coatings, and thermal management applications.

Boehmite, a monohydrate phase of alumina, offers excellent thermal stability and helps improve safety and durability in battery systems. High-purity alumina grades with optimized particle distributions enable improved thermal conductivity and efficient packing.

These materials contribute to improved energy density, safety, and long-term battery performance.

---

## Key Benefits

- Strong technical expertise and application support
- Ability to develop customized products according to application requirements
- Excellent thermal stability and thermal conductivity
- Continuous innovation for advanced battery technologies

---

## Our Offerings

### Ultra Fine Boehmite

High purity boehmite designed for EV battery applications, enhancing safety, durability, and performance in advanced battery systems.

---

### Reactive Alumina

Highly reactive fine alumina developed for thin-film separator coatings used in lithium-ion batteries.

These materials improve separator strength and thermal resistance.

### Precipitated Hydrate

# Water Treatment Solutions
