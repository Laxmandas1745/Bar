Product Overview

Precipitated Silica is a high-performance synthetic amorphous silica widely used across industries such as rubber, tyres, plastics, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.

Our precipitated silica products are engineered with controlled particle size, porosity, and surface area to deliver superior performance in demanding industrial applications. These materials enhance critical properties including rubber reinforcement, tyre durability and traction, abrasion resistance, UV stabilization in plastics, flowability in powdered food products, and catalytic support in chemical processes.

With consistent quality and customizable structures, our silica products support advanced manufacturing and industrial formulations.

Key Features

  • Controlled particle size distribution
  • High surface area and porosity
  • Excellent reinforcement properties
  • Improved abrasion resistance
  • High dispersibility in rubber compounds
  • UV stabilization in plastics
  • High purity for food-grade applications
  • Reliable performance in industrial formulations

TYSIL®

TYSIL® is a specialty silica manufactured in multiple grades including Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS), Precipitated Silica, and Water Dispersible Silica. It is available in powder and granule forms to meet diverse industry needs.

Key Benefits:
  • Manufactured for Excellence – Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS) produced through the rice husk route ensures optimal performance for tyre manufacturing
  • Improved Tyre Performance – Provides lower rolling resistance, better grip, and reduced tyre noise
  • Strong Rubber Reinforcement – High BET surface area improves reinforcement in rubber compounds
  • Industrial Adaptability – Advanced synthesis methods allow customization of particle size, morphology, and porosity

TREADSIL®

TREADSIL® is designed to enhance tyre performance by optimizing traction, fuel efficiency, and durability.

Key Benefits:
  • Enhances wet traction and driving safety
  • Reduces rolling resistance for better fuel efficiency
  • Improves tyre wear resistance
  • Enhances rubber flexibility and stiffness
  • Reduces abrasion loss in tyre compounds
Applications:
  • Tyre tread compounds
  • High-performance rubber products
  • Automotive tyre manufacturing

TAFOSIL®

TAFOSIL® is a high-purity amorphous silica used primarily in food and feed processing.

Key Benefits:
  • Prevents clumping in powdered products
  • Improves powder flowability
  • Maintains consistent product quality
  • High purity suitable for food and feed applications
Applications:
  • Food additives
  • Feed additives
  • Powder stabilization
  • Flow control agents
