# Pigment Green 7

## Copper Phthalocyanine Pigment Green 7

**Pigment Green 7** is a high-performance **copper phthalocyanine based organic pigment** widely used in coatings, plastics, inks, and industrial coloring applications. It offers excellent chemical stability, strong tinting strength, and outstanding heat resistance.

Our Pigment Green 7 grades are manufactured with strict quality control to ensure **consistent particle size, high purity, and excellent dispersion performance**.

---

## Applications

- Industrial Coatings
- Automotive Paints
- Plastic Coloring
- Printing Inks
- Masterbatches
- Decorative Paints
