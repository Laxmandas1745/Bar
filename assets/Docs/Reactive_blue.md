# Reactive Blue Dyes

## High Performance Reactive Blue Dyes for Textile Applications

Reactive Blue dyes are high-quality dyes designed primarily for dyeing **cotton, viscose, linen, and other cellulosic fibers**. These dyes form a **strong covalent bond with the fiber**, ensuring excellent wash fastness, high fixation, and superior color strength.

Our Reactive Blue dyes are developed using advanced manufacturing processes that ensure:

- Excellent color yield
- Uniform shade reproduction
- High dye fixation
- Excellent solubility
- Strong wash and light fastness

They are suitable for **exhaust dyeing, continuous dyeing, cold pad batch dyeing, and textile printing processes**.

# Application Methods

Reactive Blue dyes can be applied using various textile dyeing techniques depending on production requirements.

## Exhaust Dyeing

Used for dyeing fabrics in **dyeing machines or winches** where the dye solution gradually exhausts onto the fiber.

Commonly used for:

- Cotton fabrics
- Knitted fabrics
- Yarn dyeing

Advantages:

- Uniform dye penetration
- Suitable for small and medium batch production

---

## Cold Pad Batch (CPB)

In this method, the fabric is padded with dye liquor and then **stored for fixation at room temperature**.

Benefits:

- Energy efficient
- Suitable for large production volumes
- High dye fixation

---

## Continuous Dyeing

Used in high-volume textile processing where fabric continuously passes through dye baths.

Benefits:

- High productivity
- Consistent dyeing results
- Ideal for large textile mills

---

## Textile Printing

Reactive dyes are also widely used for **textile printing applications**.

Printing advantages:

- Sharp prints
- High color brightness
- Excellent wash fastness
- Suitable for screen and rotary printing
