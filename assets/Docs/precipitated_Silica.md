# Precipitated Silica

## Product Overview

Precipitated Silica is a high-performance synthetic amorphous silica widely used across industries such as rubber, tyres, plastics, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.

Our precipitated silica products are engineered with controlled **particle size, porosity, and surface area** to deliver superior performance in demanding industrial applications.

These materials enhance critical properties including:

- Rubber reinforcement
- Tyre durability and traction
- Abrasion resistance
- UV stabilization in plastics
- Flowability in powdered food products
- Catalytic support in chemical processes

With consistent quality and customizable structures, our silica products support advanced manufacturing and industrial formulations.

# Applications

Precipitated silica supports a wide range of industrial applications due to its adaptable properties and chemical stability.

## Rubber Manufacturing
Used as reinforcing fillers to enhance tensile strength, abrasion resistance, and durability of rubber products.

## Tyre Manufacturing
Improves rolling resistance, wet traction, and tread wear performance in tyres, contributing to better fuel efficiency and safety.

## Plastic Manufacturing
Acts as a UV stabiliser and anti-blocking agent in plastic films and polymer products.

## Food Processing
Used as an anti-caking agent to prevent clumping and improve flowability in powdered food and feed products.

## Chemical Production
Functions as a carrier, catalyst support, and desiccant due to its high surface area and chemical stability.

# TYSIL®

TYSIL® is a specialty silica manufactured in multiple grades including:

- Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS)
- Precipitated Silica
- Water Dispersible Silica

It is available in **powder and granule forms** to meet diverse industry needs.

### Key Benefits

**Manufactured for Excellence**  
Highly Dispersible Silica (HDS) produced through the rice husk route ensures optimal performance for tyre manufacturing.

**Improved Tyre Performance**  
Provides lower rolling resistance, better grip, and reduced tyre noise.

**Strong Rubber Reinforcement**  
High BET surface area improves reinforcement in rubber compounds.

**Industrial Adaptability**  
Advanced synthesis methods allow customization of particle size, morphology, and porosity.
