Refractory Alumina Solutions

High-performance alumina materials designed for refractory applications offer excellent heat resistance, chemical stability, and mechanical durability. Carefully controlled particle size distribution, purity, crystal structure, and surface characteristics ensure consistent quality across grades.

  • Coarse Alumina (unground grades for high-alumina cement and aggregates)
  • Ground Alumina (3–5 microns for castables, bricks, and linings)
  • Ground Alumina with Wider Distribution (ramming masses, continuous casting refractories)
  • High Crystalline Alumina (improved thermal stability, low soda)
  • Reactive Alumina (slide gate refractories, self-flow castables, shotcrete systems)
  • Sub-Micron Alumina (technical ceramics, high-performance castables)

Key Benefits: Superior high-temperature resistance, tailored PSD, and strong chemical stability.

Ceramic Alumina Solutions

Specialty alumina materials for ceramic manufacturing deliver excellent mechanical strength, wear resistance, electrical insulation, and thermal stability for traditional and advanced ceramics.

  • Coarse Alumina (ceramic aggregates)
  • Ground Alumina (reactive sintering, wear-resistant ceramics, functional fillers)
  • High Crystalline Alumina (spark plug components, insulating ceramics)
  • Sub-Micron Alumina (high fired density and flexural strength)

Key Benefits: Consistent quality, customizable PSD, and strong R&D support.

Polishing Alumina Solutions

Alumina polishing materials deliver high-quality surface finishes for metals, glass, ceramics, composites, and more.

Applications: Stainless steel, chrome plating, brass, non-ferrous metals, precious metals, granite, marble, plastics, rubber, wood, glass, and automotive polishing.

  • Hard Calcined Alumina (aggressive grinding)
  • Medium Calcined Alumina (medium-level polishing)
  • Soft Calcined Alumina (final-stage polishing)
  • White Fused Alumina (high-purity, durable polishing)

Key Benefits: Superior hardness, stable chemistry, precise particle control.

Flame Retardant Solutions

Alumina Trihydrate (ATH) provides halogen-free flame retardancy for cables, polymers, coatings, batteries, and more, meeting UL94 V0, EN45545, IS 15061, UL 546, UL 723, and ASTM E84 standards.

  • Coarse ATH
  • Fine Ground ATH
  • Superfine Precipitated ATH
  • Low Viscosity ATH
  • Surface Modified ATH
  • White ATH
  • Low Electrolyte ATH

Key Benefits: Effective smoke suppression, halogen-free safety, good thermal conductivity.

Battery Solutions

Advanced alumina materials support lithium-ion batteries for separators, electrode coatings, and thermal management.

  • Ultra Fine Boehmite (EV battery safety and durability)
  • Reactive Alumina (separator coatings)
  • Precipitated Hydrate (thermal interface materials)

Key Benefits: Thermal stability, improved safety, and customized development support.

Water Treatment Solutions

Alumina Trihydrate (ATH) supports the manufacture of coagulants for water treatment, improving impurity removal and treatment efficiency.

  • Coarse ATH (aluminium sulphate, poly aluminium chloride)
  • Dry ATH (PAC and other water treatment formulations)

Applications: Poly Aluminium Chloride (PAC), Non-Ferric Alum, Sodium Aluminate.
