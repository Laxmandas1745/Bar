Product Overview

Pigment Blue 15:3 is a high-performance phthalocyanine blue pigment (β-modification) widely used in coatings, plastics, inks, and industrial coloration applications.

This pigment is known for its excellent tinting strength, superior chemical stability, outstanding light fastness, and strong resistance to solvents and chemicals. Due to its stability and consistent color strength, Pigment Blue 15:3 is one of the most widely used organic blue pigments in the pigment industry.

Our Pigment Blue 15:3 pigments are manufactured under strict quality control standards to ensure uniform particle size, high purity, and consistent dispersion performance.
