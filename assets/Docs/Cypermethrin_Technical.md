# Cypermethrin Technical

## Product Overview

Cypermethrin is a widely used **synthetic pyrethroid insecticide** known for its strong and broad-spectrum activity against a wide range of insect pests. It acts primarily through **contact and stomach action**, disrupting the nervous system of insects and providing effective pest control.

Cypermethrin Technical is commonly used in **agriculture, public health programs, and residential pest management** due to its fast knockdown effect and reliable performance. It is a **non-systemic insecticide** that affects pests through direct exposure or ingestion.

## Key Features

- **Broad-Spectrum Insect Control**  
  Effective against a wide range of insect pests across multiple environments.

- **Fast Knockdown Effect**  
  Rapid action on insect nervous systems leading to quick pest elimination.

- **Non-Systemic Action**  
  Works through direct contact or ingestion without being absorbed systemically by plants.

- **Neurotoxic Mode of Action**  
  Disrupts sodium channels in insect nerve cells causing paralysis and death.

- **Versatile Formulations**  
  Can be used to produce multiple insecticide formulations for different applications.

## Applications

### Agriculture Crop Protection
Cypermethrin is widely used in agriculture to control pests affecting crops such as:

- Cotton
- Vegetables
- Cereals
- Oilseeds
- Fruits

It effectively controls **chewing and sucking insects**, helping protect crop yield and quality.

---

### Public Health Pest Control

Cypermethrin is also used in public health applications to control disease-carrying insects such as:

- Mosquitoes
- Flies
- Cockroaches
- Other household pests

It is widely applied in **residential pest control and sanitation programs**.

---

## Available Formulations

Cypermethrin Technical can be formulated into various insecticide products including:

- Cypermethrin **20 EC**
- Cypermethrin **25 EC**
- **Acetamiprid + Cypermethrin**
- **Chlorpyrifos + Cypermethrin**
- **Profeno + Cypermethrin**

These formulations allow flexible use across agriculture and pest control industries.
