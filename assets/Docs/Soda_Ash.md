# Soda Ash (Sodium Carbonate – Na₂CO₃)

## Product Overview

Soda Ash is the common name for **sodium carbonate (Na₂CO₃)**, a white, odourless and stable inorganic compound widely used as a fundamental industrial chemical.

It dissolves readily in water to form a slightly alkaline solution and is valued for its **high purity, consistent quality, and excellent pH regulation properties**.

Due to its versatility, Soda Ash serves as a key raw material across several industries including **glass manufacturing, detergents, chemicals, textiles, water treatment, and metallurgy**.

Its ability to regulate alkalinity, reduce melting temperatures in glass production, and facilitate chemical reactions makes it one of the most widely used industrial chemicals worldwide.

# Applications

Soda Ash is a versatile industrial chemical used across numerous industries.

### Chemical Production
Used as a raw material for manufacturing sodium-based chemicals including sodium silicates, phosphates, and percarbonates.

### Silicate Manufacturing
Used in the production of sodium silicate, which is widely used in adhesives, detergents, and foundry moulds.

### Water Softening
Removes hardness in water by precipitating calcium and magnesium ions.

### Oil & Gas Drilling
Regulates pH and reduces calcium levels in drilling fluids.

### Swimming Pool Treatment
Maintains alkaline balance and stabilizes pH levels.

### Bentonite Processing
Adjusts pH during activation of bentonite clay.

### Metal Smelting
Acts as a flux to remove impurities and improve metal recovery.

### Boiler Descaling
Neutralises acidic conditions and helps prevent scale buildup.

### Lithium Mining
Enhances lithium extraction during spodumene processing.

### Li-Ion Battery Manufacturing
Used in lithium compound production for battery cathodes.

### Solar Glass Manufacturing
Improves clarity and chemical stability of photovoltaic glass.

### Glass Manufacturing
Reduces the melting temperature of silica and improves workability of glass.

### Ceramics Production
Acts as a flux to reduce firing temperatures and improve glaze finish.

### Textile Manufacturing
Controls pH during dyeing and improves dye absorption.

### Household Cleaning Products
Provides alkalinity for detergents and cleaning compounds.

Dense Soda Ash is a high-density form of sodium carbonate widely used in **glass production and heavy industrial applications**.

- Container glass

# Safety Information

Soda Ash is generally considered safe when handled properly but may cause irritation under certain conditions.

### Potential Hazards

- Eye irritation
- Respiratory irritation from dust
- Skin irritation after prolonged exposure

(Source: Soda Ash SDS) :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

---

# Handling and Storage

- Store in a **cool, dry location**
- Protect from moisture to prevent caking
- Avoid prolonged skin and eye contact
- Ensure adequate ventilation when handling dust
