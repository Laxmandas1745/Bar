Product Overview

Cypermethrin is a widely used synthetic pyrethroid insecticide known for its strong and broad-spectrum activity against a wide range of insect pests. It acts primarily through contact and stomach action, disrupting the nervous system of insects and providing effective pest control.

Cypermethrin Technical is commonly used in agriculture, public health programs, and residential pest management due to its fast knockdown effect and reliable performance. It is a non-systemic insecticide that affects pests through direct exposure or ingestion.

Key Features

  • Broad-Spectrum Insect Control: Effective against a wide range of insect pests across multiple environments.
  • Fast Knockdown Effect: Rapid action on insect nervous systems leading to quick pest elimination.
  • Non-Systemic Action: Works through direct contact or ingestion without being absorbed systemically by plants.
  • Neurotoxic Mode of Action: Disrupts sodium channels in insect nerve cells causing paralysis and death.
  • Versatile Formulations: Can be used to produce multiple insecticide formulations for different applications.

Applications

Agriculture Crop Protection

Used to control pests affecting crops such as cotton, vegetables, cereals, oilseeds, and fruits.

  • Cotton, Vegetables, Cereals
  • Oilseeds and Fruits
  • Chewing and Sucking Insects

Public Health Pest Control

Widely applied in residential pest control and sanitation programs.

  • Mosquitoes
  • Flies and Cockroaches
  • Other Household Pests
