Product Overview

Reactive Blue dyes are high-quality dyes designed primarily for dyeing cotton, viscose, linen, and other cellulosic fibers. These dyes form a strong covalent bond with the fiber, ensuring excellent wash fastness, high fixation, and superior color strength.

Our Reactive Blue dyes are developed using advanced manufacturing processes that ensure excellent color yield, uniform shade reproduction, high dye fixation, excellent solubility, and strong wash and light fastness. They are suitable for exhaust dyeing, continuous dyeing, cold pad batch dyeing, and textile printing processes.

Application Methods

Exhaust Dyeing

Used for dyeing fabrics in dyeing machines or winches where the dye solution gradually exhausts onto the fiber. Suitable for cotton fabrics, knitted fabrics, and yarn dyeing with uniform dye penetration.

Cold Pad Batch (CPB)

Fabric is padded with dye liquor and stored for fixation at room temperature. This energy-efficient method is suitable for large production volumes with high dye fixation.

Continuous Dyeing

Used in high-volume textile processing where fabric continuously passes through dye baths. Offers high productivity and consistent dyeing results, ideal for large textile mills.

Textile Printing

Reactive dyes are widely used for textile printing applications providing sharp prints, high color brightness, excellent wash fastness, and suitability for screen and rotary printing.
